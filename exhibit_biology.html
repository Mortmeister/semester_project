Biology and medicine

Exhibits

From Cells to Cure

Take a closer look at the complex systems that keep the human body alive and thriving. Learn how your heart pumps blood, your lungs exchange oxygen, and your brain processes thoughts. Interactive displays let you explore anatomy and physiology through hands-on activities. See how the body works in harmony to support life every second.

Read More

DNA: The blueprint of life

Unlock the secrets of DNA and how it carries the instructions for building and maintaining life. Explore the double helix structure through interactive models and virtual activities. Learn how advances in genetic research are revolutionizing medicine and agriculture. Discover how studying DNA helps us understand diseases, inheritance, and evolution.

Read More

Micro organisms: Life at a tiny scale

Dive into the microscopic world of bacteria, viruses, and other tiny organisms that shape our world. Learn about the vital roles microbes play in ecosystems, human health, and biotechnology. Hands-on exhibits let you examine microbes up close and explore their incredible diversity. See how understanding microorganisms helps us combat disease and harness their power for innovation.
