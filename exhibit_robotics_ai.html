Exhibits - Robotics and AI

Robotics and AI

The information revolution is here and robotics and artificial intelligence are the science of the future. From useful home applications of AI to industrial uses of robotics, the future is here. You can even say hello and shake the hand of Rob the Robot.

Exhibits

The Rise of Robots

Discover the evolution of robots, from simple mechanical devices to today’s complex machines. Learn how robots are transforming industries like manufacturing, healthcare, and space exploration. Interactive exhibits let you control robotic arms and explore real-world applications. See how robotics bridges imagination and innovation to solve modern challenges.

Read More

Artificial Intelligence: Friend or Foe?

Explore the incredible capabilities of artificial intelligence and its impact on our lives. Learn about AI’s ability to process data, recognize patterns, and even make decisions. Engage with thought-provoking exhibits on ethical dilemmas and the future of AI. Discover how researchers are designing AI to benefit humanity while addressing its challenges.

Read More

Robotics in Everyday Life

See how robots are seamlessly integrated into our daily routines, from smart home assistants to autonomous vehicles. Interactive displays let you explore robotic technologies that simplify tasks and improve safety. Learn how robots are used in education, healthcare, and disaster response. Experience the possibilities of a future powered by robotics in every aspect of life.
