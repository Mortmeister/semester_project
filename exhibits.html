The Dinosaurs and the Dawn of Mammals exhibition takes visitors on a captivating journey through Earth’s prehistoric past, exploring the fascinating reign of the dinosaurs and the emergence of mammals that followed their extinction. Through life-sized dinosaur replicas, interactive displays, and fossil specimens, the exhibit vividly brings to life the awe-inspiring creatures that roamed the Earth during the Mesozoic Era. Highlights include towering skeletons of the mighty Tyrannosaurus rex and Triceratops, as well as immersive recreations of ancient ecosystems that showcase the diversity and complexity of prehistoric life. Visitors will also discover the dramatic events of the Cretaceous-Paleogene extinction, a pivotal moment that paved the way for the rise of mammals.

This exhibition delves deep into the origins of mammals, tracing their evolutionary journey from small, shrew-like creatures that coexisted with dinosaurs to the diverse and adaptable species that emerged in the aftermath of the dinosaurs’ extinction. By examining fossil evidence and advanced reconstructions, the exhibit reveals how mammals evolved in response to dramatic environmental changes, developing new survival strategies and traits. Engaging multimedia displays and hands-on activities further enrich the experience, making it an educational adventure for visitors of all ages. Whether you’re a budding paleontologist or simply curious about Earth’s ancient history, Dinosaurs and the Dawn of Mammals offers an unforgettable glimpse into a world long gone but forever intriguing.

Fossil displays and interactive exhibits offer a closer look at the creatures that ruled the Earth during the Mesozoic Era and the mammals that emerged afterward.
