Exhibits - Cosmology

Cosmology

Explore the wonders of our cosmos. Our fantastic exhibition, ‘The Sky Above Us’, explores the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us. We’ll locate the various constellations and galaxies that can be seen and learn a bit about the early navigators who used the stars to travel by.

Follow the journey of our solar exploration: from early Arab traders, to Galileo’s telescope, to the latest exploration of the planets in our solar system.

Exhibits

The Cosmic Journey

Embark on a spectacular journey through the vast expanse of the universe, from the Big Bang to the farthest reaches of space. Discover how stars, galaxies, and planets came to be and their roles in the cosmic story. Interactive displays let you explore the wonders of nebulae, star clusters, and cosmic phenomena. Witness how humanity’s curiosity has propelled us to explore beyond our world.

Read More

Galaxies in Motion

Unravel the secrets of galaxies—cosmic cities of stars swirling in endless motion. Learn about the forces shaping their mesmerizing forms, from spirals to ellipticals and irregulars. Dive into the mysteries of dark matter and how it holds galaxies together. With stunning visuals and hands-on activities, see how galaxies evolve and interact in the ever-expanding universe.

Read More

Stellar Secrets: Inside a Star's Life

Step into the fascinating life cycle of stars, from their fiery birth in nebulae to their spectacular deaths as supernovae. Discover how stars create the elements that make up everything around us, including life itself. Explore the incredible diversity of stars, from red dwarfs to giant blue stars and enigmatic black holes. Experience the beauty and science of our cosmos through interactive models and awe-inspiring visuals.
