Evolution

Exhibits

The Tree of Life

Trace the incredible connections between all living organisms, from bacteria to humans. Learn how evolution shapes life through millions of years of adaptation and natural selection. Interactive exhibits let you explore the branching tree of life, discovering shared ancestry between species. See how studying evolution helps us understand our place in the natural world.

Read More

Dinosaurs and the Dawn of Mammals

Journey back to the age of dinosaurs and witness the rise of mammals after their extinction. Discover fossils that reveal the secrets of life millions of years ago and how Earth’s creatures adapted to changing environments. Experience life-sized replicas and interactive activities that bring prehistoric creatures to life. Learn how the past shapes the biodiversity we see today.

Read More

Adapting to Survive

Explore the incredible ways organisms adapt to thrive in their environments, from camouflage to specialized behaviors. Discover the process of natural selection and how it leads to new species over time. Interactive displays showcase amazing adaptations, like the chameleon’s color change or the cheetah’s speed. See how life’s ingenuity ensures survival in a dynamic world.
