For kids

Young explorer

Are you a young person looking to learn more about science? Come on down to our museum, there’s plenty to see and do. You can learn about Newtonian physics from our bumper swing, or why not travel back in time and meet our resident Woolly Mammoth? Our exhibits are designed to be accessible for interested minds, so make sure you come ready to learn and explore.

We also have the Young Stars club which meets once a week on a Saturday between 10:00 and 13:00 where you’ll get to explore and experiment with our team of experts.

During the school holidays we run special holiday clubs where you can join other children your age to go on a journey of discovery. Each holiday we pick a new theme to explore. To find out more about the holiday club and how you can join, send us a message.

Night at the museum

Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays. Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready to rough it as we go exploring the wonders of science.Enjoy exclusive nighttime activities, storytelling sessions, and behind-the-scenes tours. Pack your sleeping bags and prepare for an evening filled with discovery, fun, and memories to last a lifetime.

Dinosaur park

Step into a world millions of years in the making as you wander through a lifelike dinosaur park! Marvel at towering animatronic dinosaurs, including the mighty T-Rex and the gentle Brachiosaurus. Learn fascinating facts about their habitats, diets, and behaviors through interactive exhibits and guided tours. Participate in fun activities like fossil digging and dino-themed crafts. It’s a prehistoric experience the whole family will never forget!

Register now

Virtual Frontiers: Explore the Impossible

Dive into new dimensions with Virtual Frontiers, where cutting-edge VR technology brings science to life! Travel through a black hole, explore the intricate workings of the human body, or visit ecosystems from the Amazon rainforest to the ocean’s depths—all without leaving the museum. With interactive virtual experiences, you’ll engage with science in ways you’ve never imagined. Perfect for tech enthusiasts and curious minds of all ages, this event blends education and excitement seamlessly.
