Visiting Professor of Aeronautics

It is our pleasure to announce that Prof Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be delivering 3 lectures on the development of aeronautics and where the future lies in this exciting ‘space’. This event is perfect for aviation enthusiasts, students, and anyone curious about humanity’s journey into the skies and beyond. Engage with cutting-edge research and insights into the possibilities of the aerospace industry.

Lecture Dates: Nov 20, 22, 24 at 6 PM

Energetica Exhibition

On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibition is coming to the Community Science Museum. It’s a series of installations that allow visitors to experience the power of the elements as we harness them. From solar energy powering lighting, to ‘Wind Island’ that shows how turbines are able to use and control wind to create power.

Dates: Nov 15 - Jan 15 | Included in admission
