Exhibits - Ecology

Ecology

As we learn more and more of the impact humans are having on the planet, the more important it becomes that scientists explore how to create a sustainable future not just for humans but for the whole planet.

In our ecology section of the museum, we unpack the dangers we currently face including global warming, extinction of species, and pollution. But we also focus on the positive ways we can all contribute to a brighter future.

Exhibits

Earth's Fragile Balance

Discover how Earth’s ecosystems work together in a delicate balance to support life. Explore the intricate connections between land, water, and air and their impact on biodiversity. Learn how human activity is altering this balance and what we can do to protect our planet. Interactive models and multimedia displays show the science behind Earth’s resilience and fragility.

Read More

The Power of Ecosystems

Uncover the hidden relationships between plants, animals, and microorganisms in ecosystems. Learn how every organism, from the smallest bacteria to the largest predator, plays a crucial role in maintaining life. Hands-on activities demonstrate the flow of energy and nutrients in nature. See how protecting ecosystems is essential for the survival of all living things.

Read More

Climate Change: Fact and Future

Understand the science behind climate change and its profound effects on our planet. Learn about the causes, from natural cycles to human activity, and the evidence supporting climate change. Explore solutions and innovations that are helping to combat global warming. Multimedia presentations and interactive exhibits inspire action to create a sustainable future.
